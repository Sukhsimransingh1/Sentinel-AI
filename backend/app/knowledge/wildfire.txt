WILDFIRE SAFETY

If a wildfire is nearby:

- Evacuate immediately if instructed.
- Move away from smoke.
- Cover nose and mouth with a wet cloth.
- Stay low to avoid smoke inhalation.
- Do not return until authorities declare it safe.

If trapped:

- Move to an open area.
- Stay away from dry vegetation.
- Call emergency services.
- Protect exposed skin.

If clothing catches fire:

STOP

DROP

ROLL

Cool burns with clean water.
